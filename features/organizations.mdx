Organizations are the foundation of WeGotWork. Each organization represents a separate workspace where you can manage jobs, applicants, and team members with complete isolation.

## Creating an organization

You can create unlimited organizations to manage different companies or departments:

1. Navigate to your dashboard
2. Click **Create New Organization**
3. Enter the organization name
4. Provide a unique slug (used in your career page URL)
5. Click **Create**

The slug becomes part of your public career page URL: `yourdomain.com/view-organization/{slug}`

## Multi-organization support

WeGotWork allows you to manage multiple organizations from a single account. This is useful for:

- Managing multiple companies
- Separating departments within a company
- Running hiring agencies with multiple clients

You can quickly switch between organizations using the organization selector in the navigation.

## Organization settings

Only organization **owners** can access and modify organization settings. You can customize:

### Basic information

- **Name**: The display name for your organization
- **Slug**: The URL-friendly identifier (e.g., `acme-corp`)
- **Logo**: Upload a custom logo for your career page
- **Website**: Link to your company website
- **Description**: A brief description shown on your career page

### Customizing your career page

Your organization's career page is automatically generated at `/view-organization/{slug}`. This page displays:

- Organization logo and information
- All published jobs grouped by categories
- Filter options by location and job type
- Branded application forms

<Note>
The career page is publicly accessible and serves as your company's hiring portal.
</Note>

## Organization roles

WeGotWork supports two role types:

- **Owner**: Full access to all features, settings, and member management
- **Member**: Can view and manage jobs and applicants, but cannot modify organization settings or manage team members

## Switching organizations

When you belong to multiple organizations:

1. Click the organization selector in the navigation
2. Select the organization you want to work with
3. Your current organization is stored in your session

All subsequent actions apply to the currently selected organization.

## Deleting an organization

<Warning>
Deleting an organization is permanent and will remove all associated jobs, applicants, and data.
</Warning>

To delete an organization:

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the danger zone
3. Click **Delete Organization**
4. Confirm the deletion

Only organization owners can delete organizations.
