## Free plan

The Free plan is perfect for small teams or organizations just getting started with hiring.

**Price**: $0/month (Free forever)

**Features**:
- 1 Organization
- 3 Jobs
- 25 Total applicants
- WeGotWork branding on career pages
- Basic applicant tracking

**Limitations**:
- No candidate CV upload
- Limited to 25 total applicants across all jobs

<Note>
  The Free plan is ideal for testing WeGotWork or managing a small number of open positions.
</Note>

## Teams plan

The Teams plan unlocks unlimited hiring capabilities for growing organizations.

**Price**: $50/month

**Features**:
- 5 Organizations
- **Unlimited** jobs
- **Unlimited** applicants
- Candidate CV upload
- No WeGotWork branding (white-label career pages)
- Full applicant tracking system
- Custom pipelines
- Team collaboration
- Evaluation and scoring
- Activity logs

**What you get**:
- Manage up to 5 separate organizations from one account
- Post as many job openings as you need
- Accept unlimited applications
- Upload and store candidate resumes
- Remove WeGotWork branding for a professional look

<Tip>
  The Teams plan is recommended for organizations actively hiring for multiple positions or those who need professional, branded career pages.
</Tip>

## Managing your subscription

### Accessing billing settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to billing">
    From your organization dashboard, click **Billing** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="View current plan">
    Your current plan and usage will be displayed at the top of the page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Only organization owners can access billing settings. Members will see an "Unauthorized" message.
</Warning>

### Upgrading to Teams

To upgrade from the Free plan to Teams:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to billing">
    Navigate to the billing page from your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Teams plan">
    Click the upgrade button for the Teams plan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Enter your payment information and confirm the subscription
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your upgraded features will be available immediately after payment confirmation.

### Viewing pricing

You can view current pricing at any time:

1. Visit the public pricing page at `/pricing`
2. Compare plan features side-by-side
3. Sign up or upgrade directly from the pricing page

<Info>
  Pricing is billed monthly. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the billing page.
</Info>

## Usage limits

### Free plan limits

When you reach the limits on the Free plan:

- **Jobs**: After posting 3 jobs, you'll need to delete an existing job or upgrade to post more
- **Applicants**: After receiving 25 total applicants, new applications will be blocked until you upgrade
- **CV uploads**: Applicants cannot upload resumes on the Free plan

<Tip>
  Monitor your usage in the billing dashboard to avoid hitting limits during active hiring campaigns.
</Tip>

### Teams plan limits

The Teams plan has generous limits:

- **Organizations**: Up to 5 separate organizations
- **Jobs**: No limit
- **Applicants**: No limit
- **CV uploads**: Enabled for all applicants

If you need more than 5 organizations, contact support for custom enterprise pricing.

## Billing access control

Billing features are restricted based on your role:

**Organization owners** can:
- View billing details
- Upgrade or downgrade plans
- Update payment methods
- Cancel subscriptions
- View invoices

**Organization members** cannot:
- Access billing settings
- Make subscription changes
- View payment information

<Note>
  To change billing settings, you must be an organization owner. Ask an existing owner to update your role if needed.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans at any time?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the billing dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed Free plan limits?">
    If you hit the job limit (3 jobs), you won't be able to post new jobs until you delete existing ones or upgrade. If you hit the applicant limit (25 total), new applications will be blocked until you upgrade to Teams.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I manage multiple organizations on one subscription?">
    Yes! The Teams plan allows you to manage up to 5 separate organizations from a single account and subscription.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a refund policy?">
    Contact support for information about refunds and cancellations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer enterprise plans?">
    If you need more than 5 organizations or have custom requirements, contact the WeGotWork team for enterprise pricing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
