WeGotWork's applicant tracking system helps you manage every application from submission to hire. Track candidate progress, review resumes, and collaborate with your team.

## Viewing applicants

Access applicants in multiple ways:

### All applicants

Navigate to **Applicants** to see all applications across your organization:

- View applicants from all jobs
- Filter by status or job
- Sort by application date
- Quick access to candidate details

### Job-specific applicants

View applicants for a specific job:

1. Navigate to **Jobs**
2. Click on a job
3. View the applicant list for that position

This shows only applications submitted for the selected job.

## Applicant information

Each applicant profile includes:

### Basic details

- **Name**: Candidate's full name
- **Email**: Contact email address
- **Application date**: When they applied
- **Job**: Position they applied for

### Application materials

- **Resume**: Download or view the uploaded resume
- **Custom responses**: Answers to job-specific questions
- **Social profiles**: Optional links to:
  - LinkedIn profile
  - Twitter/X handle
  - GitHub account
  - Personal website

### Application status

Track where each candidate is in your process.

## Applicant statuses

WeGotWork uses six standard statuses to track candidate progress:

### SUBMITTED

The initial status when an application is received:

- Application just submitted
- Awaiting initial review
- Automatically assigned to new applications

### UNDERREVIEW

Application is being actively reviewed:

- Screening in progress
- Team reviewing resume and responses
- Evaluating fit for the role

### INTERVIEW

Candidate has progressed to interviews:

- Scheduling or conducting interviews
- Multiple interview rounds
- Team evaluations in progress

### OFFER

Offer has been extended:

- Offer sent to candidate
- Negotiating terms
- Awaiting candidate decision

### REJECTED

Candidate was not selected:

- Not a fit for the role
- Application declined
- Position filled with another candidate

<Tip>
Add a rejection reason in your internal notes to improve future hiring decisions.
</Tip>

### HIRED

Candidate accepted and joined:

- Offer accepted
- Onboarding scheduled or completed
- Successfully filled position

## Changing applicant status

Update status as candidates progress:

1. Open the applicant profile
2. Click the status dropdown
3. Select the new status
4. Status updates immediately

Status changes are logged in the activity timeline.

## Resume management

Resumes are securely stored and managed:

### Uploading resumes

Candidates upload resumes during application:

- Accepts common formats (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
- Files stored securely in cloud storage
- Unique resume key generated for each upload

### Accessing resumes

View or download resumes from the applicant profile:

1. Navigate to the applicant
2. Click **CV** or **View Resume**
3. Resume opens in a new tab or downloads

All team members with access to the organization can view resumes.

<Note>
Resume URLs are secure and require authentication. They cannot be accessed without proper permissions.
</Note>

## Activity tracking

WeGotWork automatically logs candidate interactions:

- **Status changes**: When and who changed the status
- **Stage movements**: Pipeline stage transitions
- **Evaluations**: Interview feedback and scores
- **Communications**: Email and notes

View the complete activity timeline on each applicant's profile.

## Evaluations and feedback

Team members can evaluate candidates:

### Adding evaluations

1. Open an applicant profile
2. Navigate to the current pipeline stage
3. Add an evaluation with:
   - **Score**: 1-5 rating scale
   - **Feedback**: Detailed comments
   - **Stage**: Which interview or stage this applies to

### Viewing evaluations

See all team member feedback in one place:

- Average score across evaluations
- Individual interviewer comments
- Stage-specific feedback

This helps make collaborative hiring decisions.

## Communications

Track all candidate communications:

### Communication types

- **EMAIL**: Email messages sent to candidate
- **NOTE**: Internal notes visible only to your team
- **CALL**: Call logs and summaries

### Adding communications

1. Open applicant profile
2. Click **Add Note** or **Log Email**
3. Enter content
4. Save to timeline

Communications provide context for hiring decisions.

## Filtering and search

Quickly find applicants:

- **Filter by status**: Show only INTERVIEW or OFFER candidates
- **Filter by job**: View applicants for specific positions
- **Sort by date**: See newest or oldest applications first
- **Search**: Find by name or email

Filters help you focus on candidates needing attention.

## Bulk actions

Perform actions on multiple applicants:

- Change status for multiple candidates
- Export applicant data
- Send bulk emails

Bulk actions save time when managing many applications.

## Applicant lifecycle

A typical applicant journey:

1. **Candidate applies** → Status: SUBMITTED
2. **Recruiter reviews** → Status: UNDERREVIEW
3. **Pass screening** → Status: INTERVIEW
4. **Interview rounds** → Team adds evaluations
5. **Decision made** → Status: OFFER, REJECTED, or HIRED
6. **Archive** → Move to appropriate final status

This workflow can be customized using pipelines and stages.
