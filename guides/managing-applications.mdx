This guide covers how to manage applications, review applicant information, and move candidates through your hiring process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access applicants">
    Navigate to the **Applicants** section from your dashboard sidebar. This shows all applications across all your job postings.

    <Tip>
      You can also view applicants for a specific job by clicking on the job first, then viewing its applicants.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter by job">
    To view applicants for a specific job:
    
    1. Click **Applicants** in the sidebar
    2. Select the job you want to review
    3. All applications for that job will be displayed

    Each applicant card shows:
    - Applicant name
    - When they applied (e.g., "applied 2 days ago")
    - Their motivation/cover letter
    - Email address
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review applicant details">
    To view complete applicant information:

    1. Click **View Details** on any applicant card
    2. This opens the applicant detail page showing:
       - Full name
       - Contact information
       - Application date
       - Resume/CV (if uploaded)

    <Note>
      Click the **CV** button to download or view the applicant's resume.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact applicants">
    You can easily contact applicants directly:

    - Click on the email address shown on the applicant card
    - This opens your default email client with the applicant's email pre-filled
    - Send your message to schedule interviews or request more information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download resumes">
    To access an applicant's resume:

    1. Open the applicant detail page by clicking **View Details**
    2. Click the **CV** button to download their resume
    3. The resume opens in a new tab or downloads to your device
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track application timeline">
    Each application displays when it was submitted relative to the current time:
    
    - "applied 3 hours ago"
    - "applied 2 days ago"
    - "applied 1 week ago"

    This helps you prioritize recent applications and maintain timely responses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Moving applicants through stages

If you're using pipelines with custom stages, you can move applicants through your hiring workflow:

1. Access your pipeline board from the job or pipeline view
2. Drag and drop applicant cards between stages
3. Track progress as candidates move from Applied → Interview → Offer

<Info>
  Learn more about creating custom pipelines in the [Setting up pipelines](/guides/setting-up-pipelines) guide.
</Info>

## Application organization tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sort by application date">
    Applications are automatically sorted by submission date, with the oldest applications appearing first. This ensures you review applications in the order they were received.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Filter by job posting">
    Use job-specific applicant views to focus on candidates for particular roles. This is especially helpful when managing multiple open positions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Regular review schedule">
    Set up a regular schedule to review new applications (e.g., daily or every few days) to maintain candidate engagement and show responsiveness.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
